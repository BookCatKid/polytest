PolyEditor

Made by cwcinc, ported to PML by CRJakob and 0rangy

This Mod is a port of cwcinc's Advanced PolyEditor Mod


Usage


Area selection

To select an area, place the blue pillar in the lower left corner of the area you want to select

Then place the pink pillar in the upper right corner of the area you want to select

The color of the blue pillar defines what color the pasted blocks will have


Copying and Pasting

To copy the selected area, press the clipboard button on the left of the editor UI or press the copy keybind(Default keybind is C, editable in settings)

Copied content will carry over between tracks, so you can copy buildings or track parts from other tracks to yours

To paste, place the white-red pillar. You can also press the quick paste keybind(Default keybind is L, editable in settings)


Deleting

To delete all blocks in the selected area, press the Delete Selection keybind(Default keybind is B, editablle in settings)


Undoing

To undo the last action, press the undo keybind(Default keybind is V, editable in settings)
